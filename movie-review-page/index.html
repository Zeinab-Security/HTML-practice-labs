Inception

A skilled thief who steals secrets from people's dreams is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased, in exchange for planting an idea into a target's subconscious instead of stealing one. Directed by Christopher Nolan, this 2010 sci-fi thriller is known for its layered dream-within-a-dream structure, striking visuals, and Hans Zimmer's iconic score.
